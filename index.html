My HTML Cheat Sheet

Headings

Headings from `h1` to `h6` identify blocks and sections. Every page must have one `h1`.

Paragraphs

Paragraphs of text go in `p` tags.

Lists

Unordered Lists

Unordered lists are marked with bullet points that go in the `ul` tags. Each list item goes in the `li` tags.

Ordered Lists

Ordered lists are numbered and go in `ol` tags. Each list item goes in an `li` tag.

Types of Lists

  • Unordered (`ul`)
  • Ordered (`li`)

Anchors

Anchor tags create links to other pages. URL goes in `href` attribute.
